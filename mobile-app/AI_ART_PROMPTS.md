## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Consistency**: Always include "kawaii", "pastel colors", "mobile game art" in prompts
2. **Transparent Backgrounds**: Add "--no background" or "transparent background" for assets
3. **Upscaling**: Generate at 2x intended size, then downscale for crisp edges
4. **Variations**: Use "--v 5" or later for better stylization
5. **Aspect Ratio**: Match to final asset dimensions
6. **Seed**: When you get a good result, save the seed for consistency

### Negative Prompts (add when needed)
```
--no realistic, photographic, dark, scary, violent, blood, adult content, sharp edges, harsh shadows, neon colors
```
