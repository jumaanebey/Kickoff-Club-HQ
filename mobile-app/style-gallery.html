Style Direction

Critical Change

✕ Current Style (Reject)

  • Dark, saturated, high-contrast colors
  • Sharp angles, aggressive edges
  • Gritty, weathered, battle-worn textures
  • Realistic body proportions
  • Industrial, imposing, fortress-like buildings
  • Aggressive, intimidating expressions
  • Clash of Clans / war game aesthetic
  • Masculine, competitive-only appeal

✓ Target Style (Required)

  • Soft pastels, warm tones, low contrast
  • Rounded corners on everything
  • Clean, smooth, polished surfaces
  • Chibi/kawaii proportions (big head, small body)
  • Cozy cottage-style, welcoming buildings
  • Friendly, cheerful expressions
  • Animal Crossing / Hay Day aesthetic
  • Broad appeal including female players
