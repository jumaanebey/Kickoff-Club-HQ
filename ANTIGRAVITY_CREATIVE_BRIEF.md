# Kickoff Club - Creative Brief for Antigravity

**Project:** Kickoff Club Mobile App
**Date:** December 7, 2025
**Version:** 2.0 - Art Direction Update

---

## Overview

We're pivoting the visual style of Kickoff Club to be more **approachable, cute, and inclusive**. The current assets feel too masculine and aggressive for our target audience. We want a "cozy sports" aesthetic that appeals to a broader demographic, especially female players who might not typically engage with sports games.

---

## The Vision: "Cozy Sports"

**One-liner:** Animal Crossing meets Football

We want players to feel like they're building a **charming team clubhouse**, not a military base. The vibe should be:
- Warm and inviting
- Playful and fun
- Cute without being childish
- Sporty but soft

---

## Target Audience

- Age: 16-35
- Gender: All genders (with particular attention to female appeal)
- Gaming experience: Casual to mid-core
- Comparable audiences: Hay Day, Township, Animal Crossing Pocket Camp players

## Character Design: Training Units

### Style: Chibi/Super-Deformed (SD)

**Proportions:**
- Head: 1/3 of total height (oversized)
- Body: 2/3 of total height (compact)
- Big expressive eyes visible through helmet
- Small hands and feet
- Rounded, soft silhouettes

**Expressions:**
- Determined but friendly (not angry)
- Celebrating, cheering, high-fiving
- Occasional cute confusion or surprise
- NO aggressive, intimidating, or scary expressions

**Uniforms:**
- Bright, candy-colored team colors
- Soft fabric appearance (not armored)
- Rounded helmet shapes
- Visible personality through accessories (headbands, stickers on helmets)

### Unit States

| State | Pose/Expression | Visual Cues |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Idle** | Relaxed, maybe stretching or chatting | Soft breathing animation, friendly stance |
| **Training** | Focused but happy, working hard | Sweat drops (cute style), determination sparkles |
| **Ready** | Excited, pumped up, celebrating | Sparkle effects, maybe jumping, big smile |

## Building Design

### General Principles
- Rounded corners on EVERYTHING
- Warm wood and painted surfaces (not metal/concrete)
- Cozy details: flower boxes, bunting, string lights
- Welcoming entrances (not imposing)
- Nature integration: trees, bushes, flowers nearby
- Warm interior lighting visible through windows

## Animation Guidelines

- **Timing:** Bouncy, springy (not stiff or aggressive)
- **Easing:** Ease-out for playful feel
- **Effects:** Sparkles, hearts, stars (not explosions, fire)
- **Transitions:** Soft fades, gentle slides (not harsh cuts)
- **Idle animations:** Gentle breathing, occasional blink, subtle movement
